Task


Part 3 of the experiment consists of three questions that vary in difficulty.

To answer each question, type your answer into the blank box provided for that question.

Payment


This is the method that is used to determine how much you earn in Part 3.

You will receive 50 points for each correct answer. All three questions are eligible for payment, so if you answer all three questions correctly, you will receive 150 points in Part 3. There is no feedback after each question about the result. At the end of the experiment, the amount of points you earned in Part 3 will be shown on the screen. The points will be exchanged to money. 100 points are worth 1 dollar.


Click the button to begin Part 3.
